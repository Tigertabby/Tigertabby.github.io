The dark side of Sir Francis

Sir Francis is still considered one of the great economists of his time. With investments in the Hoover Dam, the Panama Canal and the Australian Oil Corp. he made billions. But was he really the charming, confident and above all honest person that those around him thought he was? Based on many years of research, this film was shot by Ruth Melspring in Croydon in 2020. A captivating drama gives us an insight into the dark machinations and the depths of the financial world.

Main Actors

Dick O'Reilly

Acting as Sir Francis. Dick became a star overnight through the series 'Investigations in the Darknet'. After 'An excursion to Galaxy II', this is his second film with star director Flynn Morg.

Suzanne Carter

Acting as Laura Francis, Sir Francis' second wife. It is her film debut after completing her model career and since then the press has printed more articles about her than ever before.

Tobb Bruce

This pic shows him in the film excerpt from the thriller "And the clock struck four." He plays Reg Kenton, Sir Francis' former business partner, whom Sir Francis not only cheated out of millions, but also started a romance with his wife Rosita. Reg therefore made a major contribution to clearing up the crimes.

Lucy Tucker

In the role of Rosita, Sir Francis' lover. Rosita's diary entries did much to clear up the real machinations. In the meantime it has also been proven that Sir Francis himself strangled her.
