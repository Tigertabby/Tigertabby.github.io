TOUCHING YOU.

A moving film about love and trust. Based on the book of the same name by J. K. Brightwolk.


THE PLOT.

Colin's life takes a dramatic turn when he loses his eyesight in a car accident. While he used to rush from one customer to another as a sales agent and hardly found time for his family, everything that he has built up over the last few years suddenly seems to have become pointless. Colin becomes depressed and has massive thoughts of suicide. In a rehabilitation clinic he met the masseuse Mary-Ann, who massaged him twice a week. With her sparkling zest for life, Mary-Ann brings hope back to Colin's life. Unlike Colin, Mary-Ann is blind from birth. But Colin doesn't yet know that he's not the only one who can't see anything ...
